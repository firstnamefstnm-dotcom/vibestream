Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how VibeStream ("the App") handles information. The App is a personal tool operated by its owner and is designed to publish content only to the owner's own connected accounts.

1. Information We Access

When you authenticate the App with a third-party platform (such as TikTok or YouTube), the App accesses only the minimum information required to publish content: your basic profile identifier (to confirm the correct account) and the permission to upload and publish videos to your own account.

2. How Information Is Used

Authentication tokens are used solely to upload and publish videos to your own account. The App does not collect, sell, share, or transmit your personal data to any third party beyond the platform you are publishing to.

3. Data Storage

Authentication tokens are stored locally on the owner's own computer in a private configuration file. No user data is stored on external servers operated by the App.

4. Third-Party APIs

The App uses the official APIs of TikTok and YouTube. Data handled through those APIs is also subject to their respective privacy policies.

5. Data Sharing

The App does not share any information with third parties. It interacts only with the platforms you explicitly authenticate and publish to.

6. Your Control

You may revoke the App's access at any time through the security or connected-apps settings of the relevant platform (e.g., TikTok or Google account settings).

7. Contact

For questions about this Privacy Policy, contact the App owner via the email associated with the developer account.
